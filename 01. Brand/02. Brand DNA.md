# ***DeskLegends Brand DNA***





#### **Brand Essence**

**DeskLegends exists to preserve life's greatest achievements as timeless collectible art.**

We believe memories deserve permanence.

Not because they happened.

Because they changed who you became.

#### 

#### **Brand Archetype**

Primary:

**The Creator**

Creates meaningful objects through craftsmanship, imagination and technology.

Values:

* Creativity
* Precision
* Beauty
* Innovation
* Quality



Secondary:

**The Hero**

Every customer is the hero.

DeskLegends simply preserves their story.

The brand never becomes the hero.

The collector is.



#### **Brand Values**

**Craftsmanship**

Every collectible deserves museum-quality finishing.

Never rushed.

Never mass produced.



#### **Authenticity**

No fake stories.

Every collectible represents a real person.

A real achievement.

A real memory.



#### **Innovation**

We combine



* AI
* Digital Sculpting
* 16K Resin Printing
* Traditional Hand Finishing



to create something impossible only a few years ago.



#### **Legacy**

Our products are designed to remain meaningful decades later.



#### **Brand Personality**

Imagine the brand as a person.



DeskLegends is:

Elegant

Calm

Confident

Artistic

Curious

Professional

Inspirational

Detail-oriented

Modern

Timeless


DeskLegends is NOT:

Loud

Funny

Childish

Aggressive

Salesy

Cheap

Flashy

Corporate



#### **Brand Tone of Voice**

Every sentence should sound intentional.

Avoid hype.

Avoid clichés.

Avoid exaggerated claims.



Instead of:

We print awesome mini figures!

Write:

We transform meaningful milestones into collectible art.



Instead of:

Best Quality Ever!

Write:

Individually crafted and hand-finished.



Instead of:

Buy Now

Write:

Reserve Your Commission



#### **Brand Vocabulary**

Always Prefer:

* Commission
* Collectible
* Collector
* Atelier
* Studio
* Crafted
* Curated
* Refined
* Museum Quality
* Legacy
* Story
* Milestone
* Preserve
* Sculpted
* Personalized
* Hand Finished



Avoid:

* Toy
* Doll
* Cheap
* Figure
* Product
* Order
* Customer
* Fast
* Discount
* Sale
* Mass Production



#### **Logo Philosophy**

The logo should feel timeless.



Simple enough to be engraved into:

* Resin
* Aluminum
* Brass
* Packaging
* Wax seal
* Business cards



A good logo should also work:

* Monochrome
* Gold Foil
* Embossed
* Laser engraved
* App icon
* Favicon



#### **Color Philosophy**

Primary Color

**Obsidian Black**

Represents:

Luxury

Gallery walls

Museum spaces

Sophistication



Accent Color

**Brushed Gold**

Represents:

Achievement

Recognition

Legacy

Prestige



Neutral Palette

Warm White

Slate Gray

Graphite

Soft Charcoal

Used to keep focus on the artwork.



#### **Material Language**

DeskLegends should always feel tactile.



Preferred materials:

Matte resin

Brushed aluminum

Natural walnut

Dark oak

Black marble

Frosted acrylic

Soft-touch paper

Premium cotton packaging



Avoid:

Plastic-looking finishes

Glossy colors

Bright saturated palettes

Neon accents



#### **Photography Style**

Photography should resemble luxury watch or automotive advertising.



Lighting:

* Soft directional light
* Strong shadows
* Black background
* High contrast
* Macro close-ups



Focus on:

Face

Eyes

Hands

Textures

Brush strokes

Display base

Accessories

Never use cluttered backgrounds.



#### **Motion Language**

Animation should feel elegant.

Not playful.

Every movement should have purpose.



Preferred animations:

Fade

Scale

Parallax

Depth

Reveal

Light sweep

Floating

Slow rotation



Avoid:

Bounce

Shake

Spin

Flash

Elastic

Overly energetic transitions



#### **UI Philosophy**

Large whitespace.

Minimal interface.

One message per screen.

No visual noise.

Let the artwork dominate.

The interface should disappear.



#### **Typography**

**Headlines**

Large.

Confident.

Minimal.

Examples:

Some moments change everything.

Become the hero of your story.

Crafted for a lifetime.



**Body**

Conversational.

Warm.

Short paragraphs.

Easy to scan.



#### **Iconography**

Icons should use a consistent stroke weight.

Minimal.

Geometric.

Prefer outline icons.

Avoid cartoon icons.



#### **Illustration Style**

If illustrations are used:

Minimal

Monochrome

Architectural

Wireframe-inspired

Never playful.



#### **Packaging Experience**

The unboxing should feel like receiving an art piece.



Inside the box:

* Protective foam insert
* Welcome card
* Certificate of Authenticity
* Collector story card
* Care instructions
* Premium thank-you note



Optional premium editions:

* Wax seal
* Hand-signed certificate
* Edition number



#### **Social Media Style**

Instagram should look like an artist's studio, not a product catalog.



Content mix:

40% Finished collectibles

20% Work in progress

15% Studio process

10% Customer stories

10% Founder insights

5% Community and behind-the-scenes

Each post should reinforce craftsmanship and storytelling.



#### **Website Principles**

The website should feel like entering a modern gallery.



Visitors should experience:

Curiosity

Emotion

Craftsmanship

Trust

Aspiration

Only then should they be invited to reserve a commission.



#### **Packaging Taglines**

Examples:

Crafted from your story.

Museum-quality memories.

Every legend begins somewhere.

More than a collectible. A milestone.

Designed to be remembered.

*We believe achievements deserve more than a certificate in a drawer.*

*Some are quiet.*

*At DeskLegends, we transform those moments into handcrafted collectible art—designed to live beside you, inspire you, and remind you how far you've come.*

*We preserve stories.*



*One commission.*

*One legacy at a time.*
