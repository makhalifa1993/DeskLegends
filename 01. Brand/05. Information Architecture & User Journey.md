# **DeskLegends Information Architecture \& User Journey**

**Purpose**

This document defines the journey every visitor takes from discovering DeskLegends to becoming a collector.

The goal is not to maximize clicks.

The goal is to maximize emotional connection.

Every section of the website must answer one specific question in the visitor's mind before moving to the next.

#### **Key Metrics**

Instead of only tracking sales, measure:

* Story section engagement
* Instagram click-through
* Commission request conversion
* Returning visitors
* Repeat collectors



#### **Mobile Experience**

Most first-time visitors will discover DeskLegends through Instagram or TikTok.

The mobile experience is therefore not a secondary version—it is the primary experience.



Principles:

* Thumb-friendly navigation
* Large imagery
* Fast loading
* Vertical storytelling
* Simplified animations
* One CTA per screen

#### **Experience Principles**



Every interaction should answer one of these questions:

* Why does this matter?
* Why DeskLegends?
* Why now?



If a section doesn't answer one of them, it doesn't belong on the homepage.
