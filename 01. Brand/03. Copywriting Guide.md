# ***DeskLegends Copywriting Guide***





#### **Purpose**

This document defines how DeskLegends communicates across every customer touchpoint, ensuring that the website, social media, emails, packaging, advertisements, and future applications all speak with one consistent voice.



#### **Brand Voice**

DeskLegends should always sound:

* Confident
* Elegant
* Inspirational
* Personal
* Calm
* Sophisticated
* Honest
* Human



The brand should never sound:

* Pushy
* Loud
* Overly emotional
* Generic
* Cheap
* Sales-focused
* Artificial



*We don't "sell."*

*We inspire.*



#### **Communication Formula**

Every piece of copy follows this structure:

Emotion

↓

Story

↓

Craftsmanship

↓

Invitation



Example:

Every milestone shapes who you become.

We preserve those moments through handcrafted collectible art.

Every commission is individually sculpted, printed, painted, and inspected.

Reserve your commission.

#### **Storytelling Framework**

Every customer story should answer five questions:

Who is this person?

What milestone are we celebrating?

Why does it matter?

How did we preserve it?

How does it feel to own it?

This structure keeps stories authentic and emotionally engaging.



#### **Writing Rules**

* Write short paragraphs.
* Prefer active voice.
* Use concrete language.
* Show craftsmanship instead of claiming it.
* Avoid excessive exclamation marks.
* Let images carry emotion where possible.
* One idea per section.
* One call to action per screen.
